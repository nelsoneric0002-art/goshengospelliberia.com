About GOSHEN

Our Vision

The name **GOSHEN** represents a place of provision, light, and safety in scripture. Our music ministry is founded on this promise, aiming to provide listeners with spiritual nourishment and songs that uplift the soul. We believe in the power of contemporary gospel music to break barriers and carry the message of hope across the globe.

Since our formation, our focus has been on creating genuine, high-quality praise and worship experiences. Every track we produce is a prayer set to melody, designed to draw you closer to faith.

Our Music and Uploaded Songs

Our catalog of **uploaded songs** spans traditional hymns, contemporary worship, and dynamic praise anthems. We are committed to frequently uploading new material, including singles, live recordings from our tours, and exclusive acoustic sessions, ensuring our audience always has fresh content.

All our music is professionally produced and available on major streaming platforms. We strive for a sound that is both musically rich and spiritually profound. You can find our newest releases and full albums in the Music section below!
